Feed Filter

Quickly filter your Instagram feed!

Ever find yourself wading through your Instagram feed wishing there was a quick way to narrow your feed so you just see the most important people you follow?

This app allows you to "favorite" some of the people you follow and then quickly filter your feed to show only your favorites.

The first step is to authorize the app to see your feed and who you follow. When you click "Authorize" below, you will be taken to Instagram to authorize sharing the data.
